About

The Greater Boston Landway map envisions a network of separated paths and lanes across the area and identifies the connections we need to build so people of all ages can get around on bikes, scooters, and other mobility devices.

Created by Zachary Yaro with Boston Cyclists Union and Cambridge Bicycle Safety.

Inspired by the Paris en Selle bike network map via Tom Babin and Scott Kilcoyne. Some icons from the U.S. National Park Service; map and website design based on the MBTA.
